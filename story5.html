Trust what you see
  • Challenge: Many perceive Tata 1mg Labs as simply a middleman connecting patients with other labs. We needed to dispel this myth and showcase our state-of-the-art, in-house facilities.
    The Hurdle: Traditionally, patients have no reason to visit diagnostic labs. They never witness our world-class labs and processes par excellence that ensure accurate results.
    Disruption: We embraced the adage "seeing is believing." Since patients couldn't come to us, we thought of bringing the labs to them! Therefore, I conceptualized and produced a captivating virtual tour film showcasing our world-class reference lab in Okhla.
    Reaching Our Audience: But how do we reach millions of potential customers with this virtual tour? Enter our innovative print ad.
    Solution: I along with my art partner brought it into a stunning full-page print ad that enticed readers to scan a QR code, unlocking the immersive virtual tour and allowing them to experience our labs firsthand.
    The Outcome: The campaign was a resounding success! Over 13,000 people scanned the QR code, and we saw a significant 10% increase in test orders within a single day.
