Ceat at Bauma 2019:
Big Tires, Big Impact
Challenge: Ceat faced an uphill battle at Bauma 2019. Joining the exhibition at the last minute, they were assigned a small, corner stall of 64 sq m with limited visibility compared to competitors like Apollo and MRF who boasted expansive spaces of 180 sqm each.
Thinking Big with Limited Space: Despite the limitations, Ceat aimed to create a stand that would outshine the competition.
Solution: Instead of settling for a generic booth design, I envisioned the hero product – a soon-to-be-launched 4-foot tire – as the centerpiece. Collaborating with a 3D designer, we brought this vision to life.
The Showstopper: Two enormous, 17-foot fiberglass replicas of the tire were constructed, transforming into the entire booth's façade. This immersive design literally enveloped attendees, making a lasting impression and drawing them into the Ceat experience.
Results: Ceat's bold approach paid off in a big way. The colossal tires captured everyone's imagination, transforming their small stall into the star attraction of the exhibition. Foot traffic surpassed even their larger competitors, with attendees drawn in to inquire about the impressive tire.
