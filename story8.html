Cloud Flier Launch for Oracle 2017
Objective: Oracle aimed to expand its cloud business with the Oracle Cloud Flyer Program, motivating its sales force to become a major player.
  • Challenge 1: Mr. Shailender Kumar, Oracle India's managing director, wished to personally launch the program in all offices. However, gathering over 3000 professionals was time-consuming, expensive, logistically challenging, and an unnecessary delay.
  • Challenge 2: Management considered sending a recorded message from Mr. Shailender Kumar to the sales force, but feared it would lose impact, as past virtual launches failed to gain traction.
  • Challenge 3: Three years before the pandemic, no reliable technology existed to meet these objectives and create a memorable experience.
Finding the Answer: To gauge employee sentiment and ideal launch format, a survey was conducted among team leaders across all 14 offices.
Conclusion: The unilateral voice was to launch the program together in person by the Managing Director.
The Solution: A Hybrid Event First
I took up the challenge and created the industry first multicity Hybrid Event of such a scale. Here's how it unfolded:
  • Three City Hubs: Replicating on-ground events at luxury hotels in Delhi, Bangalore, and Mumbai, these locations served as central hubs.
  • Nationwide Connection: We built a unique webinar format simultaneously connecting the 3 key offices and 14 sub-offices.
  • Seamless Staging: All three stages mirrored each other with a dual-LED screen setup, fostering a sense of connection.
  • Centralized Control: The event was timed to begin simultaneously in all the cities. The on-ground moderators welcomed the attendees in their respective cities. Once Oracle's MD reached the stage, the DELHI CONSOLE took over all the master consoles and remotely managed all three cities in a seamless chronological format. This enabled a seamless live Q&A session, where the MD addressed questions not just from the 3 cities but from across the 14 remote locations.
  • 2 Screen Format: The 2 screens projected the other 2 cities, and switched to presentation during the address. During the Q&A, Screen 1 projected live the person asking the question and Screen 2 projected Mr. Shailender Kumar answering the question for Mumbai & Bangalore; whereas for Delhi, the 2 screens projected the other 2 cities.
Innovation Through Prototyping:
This event format was years ahead of its time. Due to the lack of readily available technology, a custom solution was designed. To ensure flawless execution, two mock events were conducted - one internally and one with key stakeholders at the venues a week prior.
The Outcome: A Resounding Success
The Cloud Flyer launch event was a resounding success. Here's what made it a win:
  • A New Benchmark: This innovative format established an industry benchmark for engaging a geographically dispersed workforce.
  • Unified Launch: The event achieved its primary objective - a powerful, unified launch that motivated the sales force.
  • Positive Reviews: The event was met with overwhelming positive reviews from all attendees and stakeholders.
