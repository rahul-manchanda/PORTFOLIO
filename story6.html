Tata 1mg Labs:
Bharose ka Badge
  • Challenge: In a world where subpar testing is a painful reality, how do we make our customers believe in the quality of our lab reports? How do we convince customers that we are a highly professional diagnostics company when their only in-person brand interaction is through our phlebotomists?
    Disruption: What if we make our highly skilled and trained phlebotomists our key differentiator and brand ambassadors? What if we tell the world that it is not easy to become a Tata 1mg Labs phlebotomist?
    The Big Idea: With this thought, I conceived the idea of ‘Bharose ka Badge,’ featuring our phlebotomists at the center of the campaign. We showcased the dedication and expertise required to become a Tata 1mg Labs phlebotomist, including minimum educational qualifications, industry experience, rigorous training, and adherence to strict protocols.
    This campaign had two components:
    • Social Media Film: This central piece showcases a day in the life of a Tata 1mg Labs phlebotomist.
    • Print Ad with QR Code: The print ad served as a teaser, featuring a QR code that linked viewers directly to the social media film.
    Results:The campaign successfully increased brand awareness. Over 10,000 people scanned the QR code, and we saw a significant 7% rise in test orders within a single day.
